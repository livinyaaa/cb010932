CEYLON TEA INDUSTRY

Introduced in 1867, Ceylon Tea has grown to be the top agriculture export in Sri Lanka and provides direct and indirect employment to nearly 1 million people while around 4% of the country’s land area is covered in tea plantations amounting to nearly 203000 hectares. The country produces tea throughout the year and the total tea production is about 340 million kilograms per annum.

DILMAH CEYLON TEA COMPANY PLC

A team of dynamic tea traders, with a passion for "Tea" put their heads and hearts together to form Empire Teas (Pvt) Ltd. From the humble beginnings of 12 employees and a rented factory complex, Empire Teas has built itself to a family of over 125 members, operating out of a state of the art office and factory complex, facilitating annual exports of 10 -12 million kilos of tea. Empire is also among the top ten exporters of Ceylon Tea. The brand "Hyson" was born to share this passion among tea connoisseurs throughout the world.
